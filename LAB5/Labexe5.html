UNIVERSITI MALAYSIA TERENGGANU

Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is a university in Kuala Nerus District, Terengganu, Malaysia. It was formerly known as Kolej Universiti Sains dan Teknologi Malaysia (KUSTEM). The campus is located near the South China Sea, giving UMT a strong foundation in marine-based studies.

UMT focuses on research, particularly within marine science, aquatic sciences, and environmental conservation. Aside from scientific programs, UMT also offers courses in business, engineering, informatics, and social sciences.

The university continues to expand as one of Malaysia’s key institutions for marine and environmental studies, collaborating with international research bodies and contributing to sustainable development goals.
