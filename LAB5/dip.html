About Diploma Program

Is the first STEM Foundation Program in Malaysia that provides prospective UMT undergraduate students with sufficient knowledge and skills in the STEM field to enter Science and Technology-based programs at UMT.Biology, English for Special Purpose, Physics, Personality Ulul Albab, Chemistry, Mathematics, Introduction to Engineering & Technology Earth Sciences and Introduction to Programming, Science in Life

About Foundation Program

To become a policy program in the field of fisheries that is competitive and capable of providing knowledge and educational infrastructure that can be used to disseminate and apply innovative knowledge and is a catalyst for human capital development and sustainable environmental management. Produce graduates who are qualified to continue their studies at the undergraduate level at Universiti Malaysia Terengganu or at other institutions of higher learning
